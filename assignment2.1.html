Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods.Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingrdients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the cream and eggs in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted and creamand eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan tothe center every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served).
